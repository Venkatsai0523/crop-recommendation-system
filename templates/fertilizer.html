Fertilizer Details for Crops

üçá Grapes - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (10-10-10)

Apply fertilizer every 30-45 days to boost fruit quality. Avoid excessive nitrogen.

Usage: 2 kg per plant, split in 3 doses

Gap Between Uses: 30-45 days

ü•≠ Mango - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Farmyard Manure (FYM) + NPK (15-15-15)

Apply before flowering and after fruit set to ensure proper development.

Usage: 2-3 kg per tree annually

Gap Between Uses: 4-6 months

üåæ Rice - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Urea + DAP

Rice requires nitrogen for vegetative growth and phosphorus for grain production.

Usage: 50-60 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Every 30 days

üåΩ Maize - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (20-20-20) + Zinc

Apply at early growth stages and again at flowering for optimal yield.

Usage: 50-55 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: 40-50 days

üßµ Jute - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Urea + Phosphate

Usage: 25-30 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: 30-45 days

üß• Cotton - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (18-46-0) + Potassium Sulfate

Usage: 45-50 kg per hectare

ü•• Coconut - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Organic Manure + Potash

üçà Papaya - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (12-12-17) + Magnesium

Apply at the root zone for healthy fruit development.

Usage: 1 kg per plant

Gap Between Uses: Every 2 months

ü•≠ Mango - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Farmyard Manure (FYM) + NPK (15-15-15)

Mango trees need organic manure combined with NPK to boost flowering and fruiting. Fertilizer should be applied before flowering and again after fruit set.

Usage: 2-3 kg per tree annually

Gap Between Uses: 4-6 months

üçå Banana - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Urea + MOP (Muriate of Potash)

Bananas need high nitrogen and potassium for vigorous growth and fruit development. Apply fertilizer in split doses to maximize absorption.

Usage: 250g Urea & 200g MOP per plant

Gap Between Uses: Every 20-30 days

üçé Pomegranate - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (17-17-17) + Zinc Sulfate

Pomegranates require balanced nutrition, especially zinc, for proper fruit setting. Apply fertilizers in two main doses.

Usage: 1.5-2 kg per tree

Gap Between Uses: 3-4 months

üå± Lentil - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: DAP (Di-ammonium Phosphate) + Rhizobium Culture

Lentils need phosphorus-rich fertilizers for root development and rhizobium for nitrogen fixation. Apply at sowing time.

Usage: 20-25 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Once per season

üåæ Blackgram - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Urea + Phosphate

Blackgram benefits from phosphorus for root growth and moderate nitrogen for leafy development. Apply before flowering.

Usage: 15-20 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Every 45-60 days

üå± Mungbean - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Biofertilizers + Potash

Mungbeans require nitrogen-fixing bacteria and potassium to promote growth. Use biofertilizers during sowing.

Usage: 12-15 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Once per season

üåæ Mothbeans - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (20-20-10) + Gypsum

Mothbeans require a combination of nitrogen and gypsum to improve pod filling. Apply during early vegetative stages.

Usage: 10-15 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Once every 50 days

üåø Pigeon Peas - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: NPK (18-46-0) + Boron

Pigeon peas require phosphorus and boron for strong root development and pod formation. Apply at sowing and after flowering.

Usage: 15-20 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Twice per season

ü´ò Kidney Beans - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: Ammonium Sulfate + Phosphate

Kidney beans need sulfur-rich fertilizers to enhance protein content and phosphorus for seed production. Apply before flowering.

Usage: 20-25 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: 50-60 days

üåæ Chickpea - Fertilizer

Recommended Fertilizer: DAP (Di-ammonium Phosphate) + Potash

Chickpeas require phosphorus for nodulation and potassium for better yield. Apply fertilizers before sowing and during pod formation.

Usage: 18-20 kg per hectare

Gap Between Uses: Twice per season
